Home

This CIMPA Research School aims to introduce graduate students and early-career researchers to modern developments in combinatorics and discrete mathematics and their connections to computer science and related fields.

The school will include introductory and advanced lecture courses, problem sessions, and collaborative research discussions. Participants will also receive training in computational tools such as SageMath that are commonly used in combinatorics research.

During the final days of the school, participants will form small research groups to begin collaborative research projects that may continue after the school.

Organizers

The CIMPA School is a joint event organized collaboratively by Mbarara University of Science and Technology (MUST) , Stockholm University and Makerere University in Kampala.
